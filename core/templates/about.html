Behind the Magic

How we use Artificial Intelligence to find your next favorite movie.

üß† The Algorithm

This isn't just a database; it's a smart recommendation engine. We use a technique called Content-Based Filtering.

When you select a movie, our AI analyzes:

  • Genres (Action, Horror, etc.)
  • Keywords (Plot details, vibes)
  • Cast & Crew (Directors, Actors)

It then calculates the "Cosine Similarity" (mathematical distance) between that movie and 5,000+ others to find the perfect matches.
